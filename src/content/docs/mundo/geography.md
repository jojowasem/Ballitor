# Geography of Ballis

The world of Ballis is divided into three major continents, each separated by the turbulent waters of the **Shattered Sea**.

## The Continents

### 1. Eldoria
A temperate land of sprawling forests, rolling hills, and ancient stone ruins. Eldoria is the most densely populated continent and is home to the central campaign hub, [Ballis City](../provincias/ballis-city).

### 2. The Ashen Wastes
A volcanic landmass in the south, covered in black sand and active vents. It is home to mineral-rich mines and dangerous fire-attuned creatures.

### 3. Vaeloria
An untamed northern continent covered in permafrost, towering glaciers, and massive pine forests. Few cross the waters to Vaeloria due to the fierce sea monsters and harsh winter storms.

## The Shattered Sea
The central body of water connecting the continents. Legend says it was formed during the *Cataclysm of the Stars*, when a falling meteor shattered a once-unified landmass into pieces.
