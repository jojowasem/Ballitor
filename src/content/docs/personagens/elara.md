# Elara the Whispering Blade

<img src="/Ballitor/public/images/elara-portrait.png" alt="Elara the Whispering Blade" style="float: right; margin-left: 20px; max-width: 250px; border-radius: 8px; box-shadow: 0 4px 8px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.2);" />

* **Race:** Half-Elf
* **Class:** Rogue (Assassin) / Warlock
* **Affiliation:** [The Shadow Guild](../casas/shadow-guild)
* **Primary Location:** [Ballis City](../provincias/ballis-city)

**Elara** is one of the most feared and respected figures in the underworld of Eldoria. Operatives know her as "The Whispering Blade" because she has never been heard initiating an attack—only the whisper of steel before her target falls.

## Biography
Born in the slums of [Ballis City](../provincias/ballis-city), Elara learned to survive by stealing from wealthy merchants. At the age of fifteen, she was caught by the guildmaster of [The Shadow Guild](../casas/shadow-guild), who saw potential in her speed and stealth. Instead of turning her in, he trained her as an assassin.

## Current Role
Elara currently operates out of the *Drowned Rat Tavern* in the docks district of Ballis City. She manages the guild's spy network and is the primary contact for adventurers seeking work of... questionable legality.

> "Information is like water. It flows everywhere, and if you know how to listen, it will tell you where the cracks are."
