# The Shadow Guild

* **Leader:** Unknown (referred to as "The Blind King")
* **Headquarters:** Hidden beneath [Ballis City](../provincias/ballis-city)
* **Allies:** Local smugglers, corrupt watch captains
* **Enemies:** The Royal Guard, The Order of the Dawn

**The Shadow Guild** controls all illegal activities in Eldoria, from smuggling and extortion to high-profile espionage. They operate from a hidden network of tunnels known as *The Underbelly*.

## Key Members
*   **The Blind King**: The elusive leader of the guild. No one has seen his face, and his orders are delivered via wax-sealed letters.
*   **[Elara the Whispering Blade](../personagens/elara)**: Chief informant and master of the spy network. She recruits external agents for sensitive operations.

## Operations
The guild is structured into localized cells. Each cell runs a specific district or trade route. Their primary source of revenue is smuggling rare minerals from the *Ashen Wastes* into the markets of [Ballis City](../provincias/ballis-city).

## Rumors and Hooks
*   **The Golden Key**: Rumor has it the guild recently stole a vault key belonging to the Royal Treasury.
*   **The Betrayer**: A guild member has reportedly begun feeding info to the Royal Guard, and Elara is looking for outsiders to track down the mole.
